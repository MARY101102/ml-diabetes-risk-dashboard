Confusion Matrix Values:

Metric Interpretation:
- Accuracy is not the only selection metric because the dataset is imbalanced.
- Recall, F1-score and ROC-AUC are more useful for understanding performance on the positive class.
- False negatives are important to monitor because they represent positive-class records predicted as negative.

Responsible Use Note:
- The model predicts the dataset label for prediabetes or diabetes.
- The dashboard must not display the output as a confirmed medical diagnosis.
- The result should be shown as an estimated diabetes-related risk or model probability of the positive dataset class.
